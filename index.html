Technical and Analytical Skills

Business analysts must possess a variety of technical and analytical skills to properly assess and solve business challenges. They must understand how to use data to generate insights, how to develop and use models to analyze data, and how to determine the best course of action based on their findings.

Communication and Collaboration Skills

Business analysts must be able to communicate and collaborate effectively with other stakeholders in order to identify and solve business problems. They must be adept at listening, asking questions, and presenting ideas in an organized and meaningful way.

Problem-Solving and Critical Thinking Skills

Business analysts must be able to think critically in order to identify root causes of problems and develop innovative solutions. They must be able to define problems and develop strategies for solving them in a timely and cost-effective manner.
